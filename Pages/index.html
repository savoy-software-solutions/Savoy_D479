Things to know before your trip

Local Currency

Taniti uses the US dollar as its national currency. Many businesses found across the island accept both the euro and yen as well. Of course, major credit cards are always accepted during your stay in Taniti.

Learn More

Medical

Taniti is home to Taniti National Hospital, with expertly trained doctors and a multilingual staff. Many clinics can be found across the island for those unforseen accidents.

Learn More

Spoken Languages

Much of the youth in Taniti are fluent in English, while many of the older generations, especially across the rural areas of the island speak very little English.

Learn More

Power Outlet

Power outlets in Taniti are all standardized 120 volt outlets, similar to those found in the United States.
