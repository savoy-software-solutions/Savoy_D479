Where memories are made

Local Restaurant

Whether you are interested in local cuisine, native to Taniti, or crave a taste of home, Taniti is home to 10 different restaurants.

Taniti Volcano Tours

Explore the beauty and might of Taniti's active volcano. Whether you are looking for a guided tour or take on Taniti's hiking trail through the Taniti National Park, breath taking views can be captured by this island's wonder.

Local Town Experience

Spend an entire day walking through the streets of Taniti City. Take in the unique archhitecture and rich history of Taniti as you enjoy the local food and art.

Night Club

The party never stops in Taniti. Enjoy a number of pubs, microbreweries and dance clubs. Spend the night the Taniti way.

Rainforest Tours

Explore the jungles of Taniti. By foot or zip-line, Taniti offers guided tours through Taniti's rainforest. See the natural fruits and the animals that eat them!

Things to know before your trip

Local Currency

Taniti uses the US dollar as its national currency. Many businesses found across the island accept both the euro and yen as well. Of course, major credit cards are always accepted during your stay in Taniti.

Medical

Taniti is home to Taniti National Hospital, with expertly trained doctors and a multilingual staff. Many clinics can be found across the island for those unforseen accidents.

Spoken Languages

Much of the youth in Taniti are fluent in English, while many of the older generations, especially across the rural areas of the island speak very little English.

Power Outlet

Power outlets in Taniti are all standardized 120 volt outlets, similar to those found in the United States.
