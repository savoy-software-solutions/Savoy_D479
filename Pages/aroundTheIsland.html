Getting around the island of Taniti

Public Bus System

The Taniti City public bus system runs regular routes from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.

Car Rentals

Located near the Taniti National Airport, local rental agencies offer various vehicle options to meet your needs as you travel the island of Taniti.

Private Bus

Private bus agencies provide routes across the entire island outside of Taniti City providing a first class experience as you start your adventure.

Taxi Service

Access to transportation while in Taniti City is never short. Local taxi services are abudently available for ease of travel as you sightsee Taniti City.

Bicyle Rentals

Several bike rental vendors can be found across the island. Enjoy the fresh Tanitian air as you and your loved ones coast the streets of Taniti.

Walking

Taniti City is known for its walkability. Explore on foot the rich culture of Taniti.
