Things to do in Taniti

Photo: Perry Grone

Explore, relax and cultural discoveries await in Taniti. Watch the day pass by as you soak up the sun on the white-sand beaches, find adventure in the dense rainforests or sit upon the edge of Taniti's active volcano. Cool off in the crystal clear waters or cast a rod for a daily catch. Become lost in the local art, cuisine and traditions found only on the island of Taniti. The sun may go down, but the life of Taniti never sleeps! Enjoy local pubs, microbreweries and dance by the sea. Excitement to tranquility, Taniti holds everything you look for to create last memories.
