Things to know before your trip to Taniti

Every trip to Taniti is smooth and enjoyable, especially with a little preparation before your arrival. Take a moment to understand some key aspects about the island of Taniti. Having an understanding of local laws, customs and important locations can provide a level of peace of mind any one can enjoy.

Currency

The US Dollar is the national currency of Taniti. Many businesses across the island accept euro and yen as well as all major credit cards.

Health Care

Taniti National Hospital is home to expertly trained doctors with a multilingual staff. Across the island, visitors can find many clinics ready to provide expert service.

Spoken Languages

Much of the youth across the island are very fluent in English. Across the rural parts of Taniti, the older generation speaks very little.

Power Outlets

Similar to the US, Taniti utilizes standardized 120 volt outlets.

Legal Drinking Age

The legal drinking age of Taniti is 18 years old.
